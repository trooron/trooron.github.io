1. Information We Collect

The App does not require account registration, and we do not directly collect personal information that identifies you. However, the App displays third-party advertisements through Google AdMob. To deliver and measure ads, Google and its advertising partners may collect and process certain information, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Identifier for Advertisers, IDFA)
  • Approximate (coarse) location
  • Information about your interaction with ads and the App (usage data)

See Section 3 for details.

2. Camera and Photo Library Access

  • Camera: The App accesses your device camera solely to let you take photos using the self-timer feature. Captured images are processed entirely on your device and are never sent to us or any third party.
  • Photo Library: The App requests permission to save your captured photos to your device's photo library. It only adds photos you choose to save; it does not read, browse, or upload your existing photos.

All photos remain on your device under your control. We have no access to them.

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google, acting as a third-party vendor, and its partners may use device identifiers (such as the IDFA), approximate location, and information about your interaction with ads and the App to provide and measure advertising. Depending on your consent and settings, the ads shown may be personalized or non-personalized.

For more information on how Google collects and uses data, please see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS, before any data is used to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies, the App asks for your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You can change this choice at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. If you do not allow tracking, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized.

5. Consent for Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the App presents a consent message (using Google's User Messaging Platform) before serving personalized ads. You may grant or decline consent, and you can change your choice later. Where required, we and our advertising partners rely on your consent as the legal basis for processing.

6. Your Choices

  • You can reset your advertising identifier or limit ad tracking in your device settings.
  • You can manage tracking permission in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
  • Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland can review or withdraw advertising consent via the in-app consent options.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising shown in the App is not intended for children.

8. Data Security

We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Any data processed for advertising is handled by Google in accordance with its own privacy policy and security practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
