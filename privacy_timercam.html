Privacy Policy for TimerCam

Last updated: May 29, 2026

TimerCam ("the App") is developed and provided by Redseven Apps (Represented by Daisuke Yamashita) ("we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require account registration and does not track you.

2. Camera and Photo Library Access

  • Camera: The App accesses your device camera solely to let you take photos using the self-timer feature. Captured images are processed entirely on your device and are never sent to us or any third party.
  • Photo Library: The App requests permission to save your captured photos to your device's photo library. It only adds photos you choose to save; it does not read, browse, or upload your existing photos.

All photos remain on your device under your control. We have no access to them.

3. Third-Party Services

This version of the App contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We do not share any data with third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

5. Data Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit data, there is no personal data held by us that could be exposed.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

7. Contact
